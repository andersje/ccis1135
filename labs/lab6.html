Lab is worth 25 points total

  1. 12 points: Create an OOWriter document, and include one page of text on any topic you like. Your page must include the following:
    • 3 separate paragraphs of text
    • A centered title, in dark blue, in a font no smaller than 18 point
    • A quotation, properly formatted, from any source
    • One of the paragraphs must be indented one inch from the right, and 1.5 inches from the left, with the first line indented an additional .5 inch
    • There must be no spelling errors on the page
    • At least one piece of clip art, from any source
  2. 7 points: Create an OOCalc document. It must have the following items:
    • 2 columns with at least 6 cells each of numeric data
    • cells containing the sum of each column individually
    • a cell containing a grand total
    • cells containing the average of each column
    • A chart in the format of your choice, displaying all the data from the columns
    • a title for the chart
    • All sum cells to be in a dark blue font
    • All text cells to be centered
  3. 6 points: Create an OOImpress document. It must have the following characteristics:
    • no less than 4 slides
    • all slides must be separated by transitions of some sort
    • there must be clip art on EACH slide
    • at least one slide must have a bullet-point list
    • slides must have a consistent theme
